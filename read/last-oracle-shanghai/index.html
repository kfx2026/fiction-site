by Midnight Quill · ongoing

📝 Midnight Quill writes urban supernatural fiction set in the neon-lit streets of modern Asia, where ancient forces collide with contemporary life.

Chen Wei runs a tiny antique shop by day. By night, she reads fortunes that always come true. When a billionaire CEO walks in demanding to know his death date, she uncovers a conspiracy spanning three generations — and her own buried past beneath the glittering skyline of Shanghai. A supernatural mystery where fate, fortune, and family secrets collide in modern China.
